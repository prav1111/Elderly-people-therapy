Why am I doing this
India is witnessing a significant rise in its elderly population, bringing with it a host of challenges such as loneliness, mental health issues, and inadequate support systems. Many senior citizens, particularly those living in rural areas and old age homes, often experience emotional distress, isolation, and a lack of regular companionship. With limited access to mental health resources and family support, their well-being is increasingly at risk. AI-driven conversational agents present a promising solution by offering consistent, empathetic interaction. These virtual companions can help alleviate feelings of loneliness, provide mental stimulation, and serve as a supportive presence in their daily lives.
